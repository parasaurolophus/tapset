tapset

Simple, no-frills app for storing and restoring combinations of device settings (audio volume, ring tones etc.) using NFC tags or QR codes.

Requirements

  1. Simple device settings UI
  2. Write user-selectable combinations of device settings to NFC tags
  3. Share user-selectable combinations of device settings as QR codes
  4. Launch the app when a NFC tag containing device settings is tapped and restore the settings it contains
  5. Scan a QR code and restore the device settings it contains

Third-Party Dependencies

zxing
Open source QR encoding and decoding library
zxingIntegration
Example code from the zxing code> project that provides a convenience wrapper around their app-specific intents
Barcode Scanner
If the zxing Barcode Scanner app is installed, this app can invoke it to capture a QR code and then will restore the device settings it contains. Note that creating and sharing such QR codes is supported whether or not Barcode Scanner is installed; the external app is only required to capture and act on them.
